How It Works

Our 4-Step Process

1. Check the Fabric

Before anything touches your furniture, we check what it's made of and do a quick spot test so we know it's safe to clean.

2. Lift the Dirt

We loosen and lift the dry dirt and grit that's worked its way deep into the fabric, before any water is used.

3. Clean & Rinse

A light, low-moisture clean breaks down stains and odors without soaking the cushions — so they dry fast, not days later.

4. Finish & Groom

We brush the fabric back into place so it looks even and feels like new once it's dry.
