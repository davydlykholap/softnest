The SoftNest Care System

1. Fiber Identification

We determine composition (natural vs. synthetic) and check colorfastness before any solution touches your piece.

2. Dry Soil Extraction

Lifting loose grit and loose dust hiding deep in the fabric backing to prevent fiber friction during wet cycles.

3. Low-Moisture Rinse

Applying high-precision microspray agents, followed by immediate extraction to clean without overwetting foam cores.

4. Hand Pile Grooming

Using classic velvet brushes, we set the fabric grain alignment perfectly to guarantee an even, luxurious sheen as it dries.
